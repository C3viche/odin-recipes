Lentils

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 bag lentils in water overnight (change the water once)
  • 3 garlic cloves chopped
  • Chopped 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 packet of spicy salami (chopped into four)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

  2. Then add the drained lentils with fresh water covering almost completely.
  3. Add all the spices and salt and cook it 25 min at high.
