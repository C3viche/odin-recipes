Hard Shell Tacos

Ingredients:

Meat sauce:
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 1 bottle (12oz) of La Victoria red taco sauce (mild)
  • 1 can of tomato sauce (8oz)
  • 2 packs of 16oz ground beef (85% lean)
  • Salt to taste (start with a 1/4 teaspoon)
  • Garlic powder (1 teaspoon)
  • Pepper (1/4 teaspoon)
Toppings:
  • 1 bottle (8oz) of Ortega original taco sauce (medium spicy)
  • 1 iceberg lettuce (thoroughly washed and chopped in thin strips)
  • 2 tomatoes (thoroughly washed and thinly diced)
  • 12 oz bag of shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream (8oz)
  • 2 boxes of 12 hard shell tacos (Ortega brand)

    • Steps:

    Meat sauce:
    1. In a large saucepan, add olive oil and set to med-high.
    2. As soon as oil is hot (before it smokes) add ground beef, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
    3. Mix and Mash ground beef while cooking until brown and no large chunks are left.
    4. Add tomato sauce and half a bottle of La Victoria red taco sauce. Cook for another 5 minutes while mixing well.
    5. Turn heat to low and cover. Simmer for another 3 minutes. Add more salt if needed and mix well.
    6. Let cool and place some of the meat sauce in a medium sized bowl for serving.
    Toppings:
    1. Dice tomatoes and shred lettuce.
    2. Place tomatoes, lettuce, cheese and sour cream in separate containers for serving.
    3. Heat taco shells in oven on a baking sheet at 375 degrees for 3 minutes.
