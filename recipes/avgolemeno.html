Avgolemeno Soup

Ingredients:

  • Chicken broth
  • Orzo (or rice)
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 limes

Steps:

  1. In a pot boil the broth with orzo for approx 11 min or the time indicated in the orzo packet (or rice).
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs with the juice of the limes and a cup of chicken broth and keep it warm until the orzo or rice is ready. Read the below instructions to incorporate the eggs into the orzo/rice.
  3. The key to making this soup is properly tempering the eggs. Tempering means bringing the temperature up...slowly. Slowly temper the egg/lime mixture (up to medium heat) in another pot while the orzo cooks.
  4. You can then whisk the egg mixture into the hot soup pot and they won't curdle.
