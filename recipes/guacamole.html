Guacamole

Ingredients:

  • 3 large Avocados (pealed and pitted)
  • 1 eighth of a red onion (thinly diced)
  • 1/2 tomato (diced)
  • 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste (start with 1/4 a teaspoon of each)
  • 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Chopped cilantro leaves (just a few)

Steps:

  1. In a medium size bowl add avocados, onions, tomatoes, squeezed juice from lime, salt and pepper.
  2. Mash ingredients until no big avocado chunks are left.
  3. Add oil and chopped cilantro, mix well.
