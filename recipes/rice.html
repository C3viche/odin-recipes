White Rice

Ingredients:

  • 3 1/2 cups of white long grain rice
  • Salt to taste (start with two tablespoons)
  • Tablespoon of olive oil

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, add rice and rinse rice a couple times with cold water to remove starch. Remove water after rinsing.
  2. Add fresh water to rice - more than double the amount of rice.
  3. Add salt and mix thoroughly to make sure salt completely dissolves. Taste to make sure water has enough salt.
  4. Cook on high until water boils then turn heat down to med-high. Boil for 5-6 minutes and remove water.
  5. Turn heat down to med-low, add oil (spread oil evenly over rice). Cover pot and cook in med-low for 5 minutes.
  6. Mix rice and cover pot again. Cook for another 5-7 minutes.
