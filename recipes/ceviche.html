Ceviche de Camarones

Ingredients:

  • Frozen shrimp uncooked and either easy peel and deveined or already peeled (2 lb)
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Teaspoon of cumin
  • Teaspoon of salt
  • Teaspoon of pepper
  • 8 limes
  • Half red onion sliced very thin
  • Bunch of cilantro finely chopped

Steps:

  1. In the blender place tomatoes (cut in four parts), garlic cloves, salt, pepper, cumin and half cup of water. Blend until there are no chunks.
  2. Place the blended mix in a sauce pan and heat it.
  3. When the mix is heated add the uncooked shrimp peeled and deveined. In the moment the shrimp is cooked, remove the pan from heat
  4. Slice the red onion thin into a bowl, and add the lime juice and one teaspoon of salt.
  5. Let it rest until the shrimp is no longer hot. Mix everything together, add a little bit of olive oil and taste it for salt
  6. Add the chopped cilantro.
