Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Tomato sauce (1 can)
  • Ground turkey (half pound is ok for a medium pirex container)
  • 1 Chopped onion
  • 1 chopped zucchini
  • Ricotta cheese (8-12 oz)
  • Chopped Parsley 2 tablespoons
  • 1 beaten egg
  • Boiled lasaña sheets as per box instructions
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. In a sauce pan add tomato, turkey, zucchini and onions and cook it at medium heat until onions became transparent and zucchini is soft
  2. Mix the egg with the ricotta and parsley making a soft creme.
  3. In the pirex container cover the bottom with lasaña sheets, then pour ricotta mix and parmesan cheese and top it off with the cooked turkey.
  4. Repeat 2-3 layers like this. Finish with lasagna sheets, ricotta, and parmesan cheese.
  5. Cook it in the oven at 350 F for 45 min.
