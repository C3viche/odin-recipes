Tortilla de Patata

Ingredients:

  • 3 potatoes (two layers in the cazuela)
  • 6 eggs
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Half onion
  • Tablespoon of olive oil
  • Two teaspoons of salt
  • Optional: spinach

Steps:

  1. Peel and wash the potatoes. Cut them into many dimes and lay them in layers on the cazuela.
  2. Drizzle a bit of olive oil, add salt, add lid, and cook in the microwave for 8 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, dice half an onion into thick squares.
  4. When the microwave is done, add the onions. Cook for 8 more minutes.
  5. Now beat 6 eggs in a bowl and add a pinch of salt.
  6. Drizzle oil on a pan and heat it up to medium heat (6 or 7). Make sure oil is uniform to prevent sticking to pan.
  7. Add minced garlic (add spinach here too if you want) to microwaved mixture. Microwave for another 2 minutes.
  8. When microwaved add the potato mixture into the eggs and mix and mash until homogenous and chunky.
  9. Now that pan is hot add mixture to pan and use flat scrapper to make sure tortilla doesnâ€™t stick to pan.
  10. Leave for one minute and flip with flat surface (plate) on top to support and towel draped over arm and slide it back in pan carefully.
  11. Flip and slide every two minutes or so (3 more times). Final time on serving plate
