Salmon

Ingredients:

  • Salmon (1 piece,wild salmon is better than farmed)
  • Asparagus
  • A few onion rings (thin cut)
  • Aluminum foil
  • Salt, pepper, paprika

Steps:

  1. Over a sheet of aluminum foil place the washed and end cut asparagus.
  2. On top of the asparagus place the salmon and add salt, pepper and paprika.
  3. Then place a few thinly cut onion rings and a bit of olive oil.
  4. Wrap carefully the salmon in the aluminum foil so it cools inside with the asparagus and onions.
  5. Preheat the air fryer to 360 for 6 min and check if cooked at the end.
