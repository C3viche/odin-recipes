Chicken Wings

Ingredients:

  • Chicken wings (12-15)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Add salt, pepper and olive oil to the wings in a bowl
  2. Preheat airfryer to 400F and mix wings well
  3. Cook in airfryer for at least 12 minutes. Flip wings half way through
