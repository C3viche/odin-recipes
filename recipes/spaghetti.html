Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

Ingredients:

  • Half a box of spaghetti noodles
  • 1 lb (or 16oz) of ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 1 jar of traditional Marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste (start with a 1/4 teaspoon of each)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp of Dried Oregano

Steps:

Noodles
  1. Use a large pot. Fill it up half way with water. Add a pinch of salt and a teaspoon of oil (this will avoid spaghetti noodles from sticking to each other) and bring to boil.
  2. Add spaghetti and boil in medium heat for approximately 15 minutes.
  3. Use a fork to pull out a noodle to taste. Keep boiling if noodle is still raw.
  4. Once noodles are cooked use a colander to drain water. Set noodles aside.
Meat Sauce
  1. In a large saucepan, add olive oil and set to med-high.
  2. As soon as oil is hot (before it smokes) add ground beef, salt, pepper, oregano and garlic.
  3. Mix and mash ground beef while cooking until brown and no large chunks are left.
  4. Add marinara sauce. Cook for another 5 minutes while mixing well.
  5. Turn heat to low and cover. Simmer for another 3 minutes. Add more salt if needed and mix well.
  6. Mix spaghetti with meat sauce and serve.
